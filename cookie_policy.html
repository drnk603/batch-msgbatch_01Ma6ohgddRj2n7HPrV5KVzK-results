What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device (computer, smartphone, or tablet) when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently, provide a better user experience, and give website owners information about how their site is used.

Cookies may be session cookies (deleted when you close your browser) or persistent cookies (stored on your device for a specified period or until manually deleted). They can be set by us (first-party cookies) or by third-party services we use.

Similar technologies include web beacons, pixel tags, local storage, and scripts. We refer to all of these collectively as "cookies" in this policy.
