Our Story

From a small boutique idea to a trusted online destination

Founded in 2015

domain began as a passion project by designer Elena Sorokina, who wanted to bring European-style lingerie craftsmanship to a wider audience — without compromising on comfort or body positivity.

Growth & Evolution

Over nearly a decade, we expanded our collections from 12 to over 300 styles, introduced extended sizing from XS to 5XL, and built lasting relationships with ethical fabric suppliers across Europe and Asia.

Our Mission Today

We remain committed to providing premium lingerie that celebrates every shape and story. Sustainability, inclusivity, and joyful design are the pillars we build every collection upon.

Company Culture

The values that guide everything we do

Inclusivity

We design for every body, every size, every background. Our collections celebrate diversity in all its forms.

Sustainability

We partner only with certified ethical suppliers and use recycled packaging across our entire fulfillment process.

Quality First

Every piece is inspected and crafted using the finest materials — French lace, Italian silks, and premium cotton blends.

Customer Care

Your confidence is our success. We offer hassle-free returns, detailed size guides, and a dedicated styling team.

A Place Where Creativity Thrives

Our studio is more than a workplace — it is a creative sanctuary. Team members bring their unique perspectives to every collection, and we actively invest in their growth through workshops, design retreats, and open collaboration.

We believe happy, empowered employees create products that make customers feel the same way. Flexible hours, inclusive policies, and a culture of respect are non-negotiable at domain.
